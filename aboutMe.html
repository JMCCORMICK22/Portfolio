Meet the Counselors!

Jennifer Williams, LCSW

Background: A Licensed Clinical Social Worker with over 10 years’ experience providing therapy to individuals and families. Ms Allmon earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Human Services from Elon University and a Master’s Degree in Social Work from Virginia Commonwealth University. Her therapeutic experience includes providing services for children, teenagers, and adults. Ms Allmon has worked in both inpatient and outpatient settings and uses a cognitive behavioral and solution-focused approach with patients. She is also well-versed in the use of Dialectical Behavioral Therapy.

Lilly Smith, LMSW

Background: I received my Master of Science in Social Work (MSSW) from the University of Texas School of Social Work in Austin, Texas. While I was a therapist at The University of Texas Counseling and Mental Health Center, I gained extensive clinical experience. I also became interested in working with a diverse population of college undergraduate and graduate students to address the many concerns affecting their personal lives and academic performance.

Leo Smith, LPC

Background: I offer over 20 years of experience working with individuals, couples and families who are dealing with eating disorders, body-image, depression, anxiety, relationship stress, substance abuse, and grief. I know that healing and lasting change is possible when you you have support and a plan that is based on your individual needs and strengths. Therapy may be short term if you are dealing with situational stress or pain. Therapy will last longer if you are seeking to change self-sabotaging coping strategies, beliefs, or behavior.
